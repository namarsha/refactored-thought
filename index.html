A writer, editor, and researcher with a PhD in the Study of Religion from Aarhus University, Denmark. I did my undergraduate coursework at Indiana University Bloomington. I held a position as Assistant Professor in the Department of Religion at Seton Hall University from 2017-2019. Now I'm looking to implement my hard-won skills in writing and critical thinking outside the academy.
